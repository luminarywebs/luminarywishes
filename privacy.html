Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. Information We Collect

At Luminary Wishes, we collect information you provide directly to us when creating a celebration page, including names, contact emails, and personalization details for the celebrations.

2. VAR System Data Collection

Our `config.js` and system infrastructure may cache standard session data strictly via `sessionStorage` (e.g., `selectedPlan`) locally on your device to ensure continuous service operations during the checkout flow.

3. Use of Information

We use the information we collect strictly to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our customized celebration web pages.
  • Process transactions and send related information, including confirmations and receipts.
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests through Telegram or WhatsApp.

4. Cookie Policy

We use first-party cookies solely to maintain state persistence across our subdomains (e.g., remembering your last selected plan via `lw_last_plan`). We do not use third-party advertising tracking cookies.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our data protection team via the information provided on our Contact page or the global footer below.
