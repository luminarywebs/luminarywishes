About

AR. Abhinav Ranjan is a cybersecurity researcher, educator, infrastructure architect, and the founder of the Luminary Technicals ecosystem. He is celebrated as one of the youngest ethical hacking educators in the world, recognized by the Guinness World Records and the Golden Book of World Records.

His work spans secure digital infrastructure, full-stack web development, ethical AI governance, and building digital awareness for the next generation.

Luminary Wishes, Luminary Teaching Labs, and the broader Luminary ecosystem are expressions of that belief — tools that illuminate rather than overwhelm.
